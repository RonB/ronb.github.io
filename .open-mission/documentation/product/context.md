`.open-mission/documentation/product/context.md` is the canonical glossary and relationship map for this repository.

Use it when a term, ownership rule, or relationship needs a stable name. Product pages summarize the terms for the docs site; `.open-mission/documentation/product/context.md` remains the source of truth.
